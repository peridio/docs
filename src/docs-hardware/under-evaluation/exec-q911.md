## Overview

The Innodisk EXEC-Q911 is a ready-to-use edge AI starter kit built on the Qualcomm Dragonwing IQ-9075 in a compact COM-HPC Mini form factor. It pairs an octa-core Kryo CPU and dual Hexagon Tensor Processors delivering up to 200 TOPS (sparse) with 36GB LPDDR5X, 128GB UFS 3.1 storage, dual 2.5GbE, and a wide -40°C to +85°C operating range — purpose-built for AGV/AMR, vision computing, generative AI inference, and industrial IoT.
