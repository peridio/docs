# ASUS IoT PE1100N

Ultra-compact 1.25-liter fanless edge AI computer powered by NVIDIA Jetson Orin

**Up to 8-core Cortex-A78AE** | **Up to 100 TOPS** | **8/16GB LPDDR5** | **Fanless** | **1.25L**

<a href="https://www.asus.com/networking-iot-servers/aiot-industrial-solutions/embedded-computers-edge-ai-systems/pe1100n/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">View on ASUS.com</a>

## Overview

The ASUS IoT PE1100N packs NVIDIA Jetson Orin performance into a 1.25-liter fanless enclosure — approximately 3% the size of a standard ATX system. It provides rich industrial I/O including dual LAN, DIO, COM ports, and multiple M.2 slots for Wi-Fi, 5G, and NVMe expansion. With onboard Wi-Fi and Bluetooth, dual SIM support, and GPS, it is a versatile platform for intelligent edge deployments.
