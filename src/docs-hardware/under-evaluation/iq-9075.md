# Qualcomm Dragonwing IQ-9075

Industrial-grade AI platform delivering up to 100 TOPS with LLM inference capability

**Octa-core Kyro Gen 6 @ 2.36GHz** | **Up to 100 TOPS** | **36GB LPDDR5 (ECC)** | **Wi-Fi 6E**

## Overview

The Qualcomm Dragonwing IQ-9075 delivers up to 100 dense TOPS of AI inference, capable of running Llama 2 7B at 22 tokens/sec on-device. It features an octa-core CPU, Adreno 663 GPU, up to 36GB LPDDR5 with inline ECC, and comprehensive connectivity including Wi-Fi 6E, 2.5GbE, and CAN-FD.
