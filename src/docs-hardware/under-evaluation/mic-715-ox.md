## Overview

The MIC-715-OX is a ruggedized, fanless AI inference system designed for heavy industry and in-vehicle deployments. It features IP67-rated waterproof and dustproof construction with M12 lockable connectors for advanced vibration resistance. The system supports simultaneous Wi-Fi, 4G/LTE, and 5G connectivity alongside built-in PoE and GMSL2 camera interfaces.
