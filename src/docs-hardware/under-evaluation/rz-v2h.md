# Renesas RZ/V2H

Quad-core vision AI MPU with 80 TOPS DRP-AI3 accelerator and real-time Cortex-R8 cores

**4x Cortex-A55 @ 1.8GHz** | **2x Cortex-R8 @ 800MHz** | **80 TOPS DRP-AI3** | **10 TOPS/W efficiency**

<a href="https://www.renesas.com/en/products/rz-v2h" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">View on Renesas.com</a>

## Overview

The Renesas RZ/V2H combines quad Cortex-A55 application cores, dual Cortex-R8 real-time cores, and a Cortex-M33 for system management alongside Renesas' proprietary DRP-AI3 accelerator delivering up to 80 TOPS at an industry-leading 10 TOPS/W power efficiency — a 10x improvement over previous generations. The chip also integrates a separate DRP engine for hardware-accelerated OpenCV and dynamics calculations. Designed for next-generation robotics, it unifies vision AI and real-time motor/sensor control in a single SoC.
