## Overview

The Connect Tech Rogue Carrier (AGX202) is a compact 92 x 107 mm carrier board for the NVIDIA Jetson AGX Orin and AGX Orin Industrial modules, built for commercial deployment. It pairs dual 10GBASE-T Ethernet with dual NVMe M.2 storage, MIPI CSI-2 / GMSL camera input, CAN FD, and a wide -40°C to +85°C operating range — purpose-built for robotics, autonomous machines, and industrial edge AI.
