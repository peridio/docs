# CompuLab EdgeAI-ORN

Rugged MIL-STD-810G Edge AI computer with flexible camera inputs and industrial interfaces

**Up to 157 TOPS** | **Up to 16GB LPDDR5** | **CAN-FD** | **MIL-STD-810G** | **-25 to 75C**

<a href="https://edge.compulab.com/products/edgeai-orn/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">View on CompuLab.com</a>

## Overview

The EdgeAI-ORN supports NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano and Orin NX modules, delivering up to 157 TOPS of AI performance. It stands out with its flexible video input architecture supporting GMSL2, FPD-Link IV, MIPI CSI-2, PoE Ethernet, and USB 3.2 camera connectivity. The system meets MIL-STD-810G standards with an operating range of -25C to 75C.
