## Overview

The ICAM-300 is a compact industrial AI smart camera powered by the Qualcomm QCS6490 with 12 TOPS of on-device AI performance. It integrates a 6mm focal length lens with closed-loop VCM autofocus, 8 programmable PWM white LED illuminators, and Qualcomm Spectra 570L ISP in a rugged IP66-rated enclosure.
