## Overview

The TPC-115W is a 15.6-inch industrial touch panel computer built on the NXP i.MX 8M Mini platform. It features a true-flat IP66-rated front panel with 10-point projected capacitive multi-touch, designed for factory automation HMI and web-based operation terminals. The device includes CAN 2.0B protocol support and PoE-PD Ethernet, making it ideal for industrial environments.
