## Overview

The Connect Tech Rudi-NX is a compact embedded system carrier for NVIDIA Jetson Orin NX, featuring an extremely small 135x50x105mm footprint. It includes quad GMSL 1/2 camera inputs with Power Over Coax, dual Gigabit Ethernet, M.2 expansion, and isolated CAN bus — purpose-built for vision-centric edge AI in autonomous machines, drones, and robotics.
