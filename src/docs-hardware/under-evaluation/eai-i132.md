# Lanner EAI-I132

Fanless industrial-grade edge AI computer powered by NVIDIA Jetson Orin NX/Nano

**Up to 100 TOPS** | **Jetson Orin NX / Nano** | **PoE+ up to 60W** | **-40 to 70C** | **Fanless**

<a href="https://www.lannerinc.com/products/edge-ai-appliance/edge-inference-appliances/eai-i132" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">View on Lanner Electronics</a>

## Overview

The Lanner EAI-I132 is a fanless, industrial-grade edge AI appliance built on the NVIDIA Jetson Orin NX or Orin Nano module, delivering up to 100 TOPS of on-device inference in a rugged, wide-temperature chassis. It pairs PoE+ networking (up to 60W across two ports) with USB, serial, and digital I/O, plus optional 5G/LTE, Wi-Fi, GPS, and a G-sensor — making it well suited to traffic analytics, PPE compliance, and mobile or industrial autonomous systems where cameras and sensors connect directly to the appliance. It is compatible with the NVIDIA JetPack SDK 6.0.
