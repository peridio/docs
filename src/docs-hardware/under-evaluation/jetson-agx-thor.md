# NVIDIA Jetson AGX Thor

Blackwell-powered robotics supercomputer delivering up to 2070 FP4 TFLOPS for physical AI

**14-core Neoverse-V3AE** | **2560-core Blackwell GPU** | **128GB LPDDR5X** | **40-130W**

<a href="https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/autonomous-machines/embedded-systems/jetson-thor/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">View on NVIDIA.com</a>

## Overview

The NVIDIA Jetson AGX Thor is the flagship Jetson module, delivering 2070 FP4 TFLOPS — 7.5x the AI performance of AGX Orin at 3.5x better efficiency. Built on the Blackwell GPU architecture with a 14-core Arm Neoverse-V3AE CPU, it includes 128GB LPDDR5X memory, PCIe Gen5, 16x CSI-2 camera lanes, and 4x25GbE networking. It is the definitive platform for humanoid robotics, autonomous systems, and next-generation physical AI.
