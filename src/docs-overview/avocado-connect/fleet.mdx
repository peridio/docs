# Fleet

Whether it's 10 devices on a pilot line or 5,000 across a continent, you know what's running, where, and whether it's healthy. Fleet management in Avocado Connect gives you visibility and control across every device running Avocado OS.

- **Device inventory**: See every device at a glance: its hardware target (surfaced as a tag), runtime version, last check-in, and health status.
- **Cohorts and tags**: Group devices by hardware revision, deployment stage, region, customer, or any attribute, then target updates to specific cohorts rather than the whole fleet. Tags give you a complementary targeting primitive: filter and deploy by tag to reach exactly the devices you mean.
- **Remote diagnostics**: Inspect system state through each device's reported state (shadow) without a site visit. Live log viewing is coming soon.
- **Health monitoring**: Track connectivity patterns, update completion, and fleet-wide trends, so you catch systemic issues early rather than reacting to individual failures.
