delete

Usage: peridio firmwares delete --firmware-uuid <FIRMWARE_UUID> --product-name <PRODUCT_NAME>

Options:
      --firmware-uuid <FIRMWARE_UUID>  The UUID of the firmware you wish to delete
      --product-name <PRODUCT_NAME>    The name of the product you wish to delete the resource within
