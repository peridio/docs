CA Certificates

A CA certificate is an X.509 certificate that signs device certificates. The validity of said signature on the device's certificate is verified when a user creates a device certificate and as part of just in time provisioning.

Just in Time Provisioning​

CA certificates that are added to Peridio can optionally enable just in time provisioning. This will allow an unprovisioned device to automatically become provisioned at the moment of its first connection to Peridio.

As part of this configuration, you define a description, tags, and product to be applied to devices that provision in this fashion.
